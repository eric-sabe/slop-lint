# Introducing Flow: The Notebook That Thinks the Way You Work

**Today, we're thrilled to announce the launch of Flow, a productivity notebook app designed to help you capture ideas, organize your thoughts, and get more done—without getting in your way.**

For too long, note-taking apps have forced you to choose. You could have something simple and fast but limited. Or you could have something powerful and flexible but cluttered with features you'll never use. We built Flow because we believed you shouldn't have to compromise. Your notes should be as quick as a sticky note and as organized as a filing cabinet—all at once.

## Why We Built Flow

Like many of you, our team spent years juggling a chaotic mix of tools. Ideas scattered across a dozen apps. To-do lists buried in documents. Meeting notes lost in endless folders. We kept asking ourselves the same question: why does staying organized feel like a full-time job?

So we set out to build something different. Not just another place to dump text, but a thinking environment—a space that adapts to how your mind actually works. Whether you're jotting a quick reminder, drafting a project plan, or connecting research across weeks of work, Flow is built to keep up.

## What Makes Flow Different

**Capture at the speed of thought.**
Open Flow and start typing. There's no setup, no friction, no decisions to make before you can write a single word. Our quick-capture feature lets you save ideas in under a second, then organize them later—because the best ideas don't wait for you to find the right folder.

**Organization that happens automatically.**
Flow learns from how you use it. As you write, it intelligently suggests tags, surfaces related notes, and groups connected ideas together. You can build your own structure with notebooks, nested pages, and custom labels—or let Flow's smart sorting do the heavy lifting. Either way, you'll always find what you're looking for.

**Powerful linking between ideas.**
Great thinking happens when ideas connect. Flow lets you link any note to any other note with a simple keystroke, building a web of knowledge that grows richer over time. Our visual map view shows you how your thoughts relate, helping you spot patterns and make connections you'd otherwise miss.

**Tasks and notes, finally together.**
Stop bouncing between your notes and your to-do list. In Flow, you can turn any line into a task, set due dates, and track progress right alongside the context that matters. Your action items live where your ideas were born—not in a separate app that forgets why the task existed in the first place.

**Built for focus.**
We obsessed over speed and simplicity. Flow opens instantly, works offline, and syncs seamlessly across your devices. A clean, distraction-free interface keeps your attention on your work, not on the tool. Dark mode, keyboard shortcuts, and customizable themes let you make Flow your own.

## Designed for Everyone

Flow works for students organizing research, professionals managing projects, writers drafting their next chapter, and anyone who simply wants to think more clearly. Whether you're a solo user or part of a team, Flow scales with your needs. Shared notebooks and real-time collaboration make it easy to work together without losing the simplicity you love.

## Your Data, Your Control

We take privacy seriously. Your notes are encrypted and belong to you—always. Export everything anytime in open formats. No lock-in, no surprises. We believe a tool you trust is a tool you'll actually use.
